FNF QT Mod

Welcome to the FNF QT Mod, a rhythm game variant inspired by Friday Night Funkin'. This mod introduces special mechanics and themed content to enhance your gaming experience.

Special Mechanics:

  • Dynamic Rhythm Changes: Experience the game with unpredictable rhythm changes that will keep you on your toes!
  • Power-Ups: Collect power-ups during gameplay that can modify notes or enhance your character's abilities.
  • Custom Skins: Unlock custom skins for characters that fit various themes and styles.

Themes:

  • Nightmare Realm: Face enemies in a dark, eerie environment with a haunting soundtrack.
  • Candy Land: Battle it out in a colorful environment filled with candy-themed characters.
  • Space Adventure: Take your skills to outer space with cosmic visuals and sci-fi beats.
